Introduction

At Nexgen Learning, we believe education is a fundamental human right, and so is privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Aurora educational platform.

We are committed to transparency and clarity. This document is designed to be readable, skipping the dense legal jargon wherever possible while maintaining the rigorous protections your data deserves.

Data Collection

person

Identity Information

Full name, email address, and profile biography used to personalize your learning journey and facilitate communication.

monitoring

Usage Metrics

Time spent on lessons, quiz scores, and interaction patterns to help us optimize the curriculum and platform performance.

Device & Browser Data

IP addresses, browser types, and operating system information are automatically logged to ensure technical stability and prevent unauthorized access.

Use of Information

  • 01.
    Curating Your Path

    We use your progress data to recommend specific editorial modules that match your learning pace and interests.

  • 02.
    Platform Integrity

    Automated systems analyze interaction patterns to detect fraudulent behavior or potential security threats.

  • 03.
    Direct Updates

    Email communications regarding course updates, legal changes, or technical maintenance of the Aurora system.
